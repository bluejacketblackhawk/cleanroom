Cleanroom bundles this model unmodified as the default diarization speaker-embedding
extractor. The full CC-BY-4.0 legal text is in cc-by-4.0-legalcode.txt next to
this file.

   sherpa-onnx project (k2-fsa)."

Indication of changes (CC-BY-4.0 §3.a.1.B): the original NeMo checkpoint was
converted to the ONNX runtime format by the sherpa-onnx project. Cleanroom does not
otherwise modify the weights.

Why this model is the default (not the Apache-2.0 WeSpeaker CAM++): CAM++ emits
degenerate embeddings under the prebuilt Windows onnxruntime combination Cleanroom
ships, collapsing every recording to a single speaker. TitaNet-Small runs
correctly. See crates/anvil-asr/src/model.rs for the full rationale. This
attribution obligation is the price of a diarizer that actually works on Windows.
